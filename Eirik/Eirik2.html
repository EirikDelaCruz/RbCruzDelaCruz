HAVEN

Haven features three maps labeled A, B, and C. Because of this, it was given the codename "triad" during its development. All three of these maps have similar features such as platforms which the players could get on. There are orbs in the mid sections of each site except for site B. Unlike the other maps in this game, there is not much lore found in this map. It has a simple layout that consists of a site littered with radianite crates and storage, a middle area, spawns, and high platforms.
